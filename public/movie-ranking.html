The Best Movies According to Me.

My "Best 3 Movies of All-Time".

Inception.

Mind-bending dream layers and stunning visuals kept me captivated throughout.

The Grand Budapest Hotel.

Wes Anderson's quirky charm and a fast-paced, eccentric plot made this a delightful cinematic experience.

Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind.

A poignant exploration of love and memory, with compelling performances and an unpredictable narrative.
